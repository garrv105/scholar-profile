arXiv:2507.00403 [quant-ph] · Cornell University · July 2025

A quantum Bayesian inference method for intrusion detection using explicitly constructed quantum circuits. Prior and conditional probabilities encoded via unitary gates; posterior distributions extracted through symbolic post-selection — demonstrating quantum-native inference for information security applications.
